Jabalpur


Jabalpur, formerly known as Jubbulpore, is a city located on the banks of the Narmada River in the state of Madhya Pradesh, India. With a population of over 1 million people, it is the third-largest urban agglomeration in Madhya Pradesh and the 38th-largest in the country.
